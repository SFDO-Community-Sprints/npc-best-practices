# Stakeholder Identification & Evaluations

A migration of this scope affects more people than most organizations initially anticipate, and understanding who has a stake in the outcome is itself an important early step. This section is organized in two parts.

The *Quick Guide* groups stakeholders into broad categories (End Users, Champions, Leadership Stakeholders, Security/Compliance, and Budget/Finance) and is a good starting point for organizations early in the conversation and/or smaller org structures. 

The *Comprehensive Guide* that follows breaks those categories into more specific personas, organized by whether they are internal or external to the organization.

Both guides use the same three-part structure: Goals, Challenges, and Questions to Ask. The goals and challenges are intended to help you understand each stakeholder's perspective before you engage them. The questions serve a dual purpose: they are a guide for the nonprofit's project lead to make sure each stakeholder's needs and concerns are being heard, and they are a reference for whoever is doing the planning or technical work to ensure nothing important gets missed.
